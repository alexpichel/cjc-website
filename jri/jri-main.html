JRI Grant

The Justice Reinvestment Grant Program provides funding for Oregon counties to plan, implement, or expand initiatives that meet the four goals of Justice Reinvestment:

  • Reduce recidivism
  • Reduce prison population
  • Increase public safety
  • Hold offenders accountable
