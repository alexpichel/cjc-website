The Oregon Criminal Justice Commission (CJC) is pleased to invite Oregon counties to submit applications for the Justice Reinvestment Grant Program (“Grant Program”).

The Grant Program is targeted to financially support Oregon counties to plan, implement, or expand initiatives that reduce recidivism, reduce prison population, increase public safety, and hold offenders accountable. Grant awards are contingent on Grant Program funding and application approval. Funding for this program is through state general fund dollars appropriated by the 2017 Oregon Legislature.
